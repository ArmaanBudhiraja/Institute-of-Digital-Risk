# Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) – Homepage & Logo Design
## Project Overview

This project presents the brand identity and homepage design for the Institute of Digital Risk (IDR), an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber, and AI risk.

The goal of this project was to design a clean, professional logo and develop a responsive homepage that clearly communicates the mission of the institute while maintaining a modern academic and technology-focused aesthetic.

The website was built using semantic HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript, ensuring accessibility, responsiveness, and simplicity without using external frameworks.

---

## Logo Design
**Concept**

The IDR logo uses a geometric cube-inspired icon to represent:

- Structure – organized frameworks used in risk management

- Resilience – the ability of digital systems to withstand threats

- Digital infrastructure – the layered architecture of modern technology systems

The cube structure symbolizes stability and interconnected systems, reflecting how digital risk management requires structured governance and strong foundations.

**Colour Palette**

The primary colour palette includes:

- Orange – innovation, energy, and forward-thinking technology

- Black – authority, professionalism, and trust

- White – clarity, simplicity, and accessibility

This combination creates a modern technology and education-oriented visual identity.

**Typography**

A clean sans-serif typeface was chosen to maintain readability and professionalism across digital platforms.
The typography ensures the logo remains clear and legible at smaller sizes, such as favicons or mobile headers.

Logo Variants

Two versions of the logo were created:

1. Icon-only version – suitable for favicons and compact branding

2. Icon + text version – displaying the full name Institute of Digital Risk

Both versions maintain consistency in colour, geometry, and visual balance.

## Website Overview

The website is a single-page responsive homepage designed to clearly present the institute’s mission, services, and community.

The design follows a minimal and professional layout, suitable for an academic or research-focused organisation.

---

## Website Sections
1. Hero Section

The hero section introduces the institute with the headline:

“Advancing the Future of Digital Risk.”

It includes a short description of the institute’s role in training and deploying digital risk practitioners while combining academic research and industry practice.

Call-to-action buttons allow users to:

- Explore programs

- Hire trained talent

2. About IDR

This section explains the purpose of the institute as an industry-led training and deployment hub for digital, cyber, and technology risk roles.

It highlights the connection between:

- Academic insight

- Industry collaboration

- Real-world projects

3. Service Pillars

The core operating model of the institute is represented through three main pillars:

**Academy**

Training programs and bootcamps for students and professionals seeking careers in digital and cyber risk.

**Innovation & Incubation**

Development of new research, intellectual property, and governance frameworks related to AI and digital risk.

**Advisory Services**

Support for organisations implementing recognised risk frameworks such as NIST, ISO 27001, and NIS2.

4. Training and Deployment Model

The institute’s approach is represented through a pipeline:

Train → Hire → Innovate → Deploy

This model demonstrates how IDR develops talent and connects trained professionals with real-world industry needs.

5. Community

The institute serves a diverse community including:

- Students and graduates entering cybersecurity fields

- Early-career professionals looking to specialise in digital risk

- Practitioners working in regulated sectors

Key industries include financial services, technology, and critical infrastructure.

6. Contact Section

The website includes a simple contact form allowing users to register interest or reach out for collaboration opportunities.

The form collects:

- Name

- Email

- Message
